Expressive truth

Main ideas:

  1. Misquotation: from “more philosophical” to “the most philosophical”——> from apology for poetry(Aristotle) to glorification (Wordsworth and other Romantics)
  2. Pays lip service to Aristotle’s idea of truth as “not local and individual, but general and operative”——>from the classical and neoclassical concern with the universal in and of itself to the Romantic preoccupation with the universal in the particular
  3. A revolutionary claim for poetry made by Wordsworth himself ——>paraphrase of Aristotle’s view: poetry conveys the truth that is its own testimony “carried alive into the heart”
